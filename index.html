How costs are computed
Base Distance
KES 0.04/kg/km. Maua-Isiolo 55km = KES 2.20/kg. Likoni routes reach KES 21/kg base.
Road Premium
Isiolo murram +KES 3.50. Coastal wear +KES 0.50. A2 Nairobi +KES 0.00.
Vehicle Premium
Probox/pickup +0. 5T lorry +KES 0.50. Refrigerated coastal lorry +KES 1.00.
Urgency Premium
Same-day markets highest: Isiolo +KES 4.30, Nairobi +KES 4.20. Coastal planned runs: +KES 0.20-0.30.
